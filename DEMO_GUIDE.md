# Meridian Demo Guide

**Prepared for:** Leo (Co-founder Demo)
**Product:** Meridian — AI-Powered Sales Intelligence Platform
**Live URL:** [meridianos.ai](https://meridianos.ai)

---

## Overview

This guide walks you through a structured demo of Meridian's core features. The product is designed for B2B enterprise sales teams managing complex, multi-stakeholder deals. The demo uses pre-seeded mock data (Acme Corp, GlobalTech, etc.) to showcase the platform's capabilities.

**Demo duration:** 10–15 minutes for a full walkthrough, or 5 minutes for a highlights-only pass.

---

## Pre-Demo Setup

Before starting the demo, ensure the following:

| Item | Action |
|------|--------|

## Demo Flow

### Act 1: The Command Center (Dashboard)

**What to show:** After login, you land on the Dashboard — the sales leader's morning briefing.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Greeting & Overdue Alert Banner** — The top banner shows overdue actions that need attention. This is Meridian proactively nudging the rep, not waiting for them to dig through CRM data.

2. **Four KPI Cards** — Total Pipeline, Predictable Revenue (deals above 70% confidence), Average Confidence Score, and At-Risk Deals. These are calculated in real-time from deal data, not manually entered.

3. **Deals Needing Action** — The left panel highlights deals with low confidence or stalled progress. Click any deal to dive in.

4. **All Deals List** — The right panel shows the full pipeline with color-coded confidence scores (green = healthy, amber = watch, red = at risk).

5. **Pipeline by Stage** — A visual funnel showing deal distribution across stages (Discovery → Demo → Technical Evaluation → POC → Negotiation).

**Demo script:**
> "This is what a sales rep sees every morning. Instead of opening Salesforce and running 5 reports, Meridian tells you exactly what needs your attention — which deals are at risk, which actions are overdue, and where your pipeline stands."

---

### Act 2: Deep Dive into a Deal (Deal Detail)

**What to show:** Click on "Acme Corp" (the highest-value deal) to enter the Deal Detail view.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Deal Header** — Company name, deal value ($240K), stage (Technical Evaluation), and the AI Confidence Score (68%) with trend indicator (+5 from last week).

2. **Buying Committee Tab** — This is the hero feature. The left panel shows the AI-generated Deal Insight:
   - **What's Happening** — AI summary of the current deal situation, highlighting key stakeholders by name and their recent actions.
   - **Key Risks** — Expandable risk cards (Budget Freeze Risk = High, Security Audit Pending = Medium) with AI-recommended actions.
   - **What's Next** — Prioritized action items with due dates, assignees, and status (In Progress / Accepted).

3. **Stakeholder Map** (right panel) — The concentric ring visualization:
   - Inner ring (blue) = Decision Makers (CEO, CFO)
   - Middle ring (green) = Champions & Influencers (Head of Sales Ops, CTO)
   - Outer ring (red) = Blockers (InfoSec Lead, Procurement Mgr)
   - Arrows show relationships: Reports To (blue), Influences (green), Collaborates (amber dashed), Blocks (red dashed)

4. **AI Chat** — The bottom input field lets the rep ask questions about the deal: "What's the biggest risk right now?" or "Draft an email to the CFO about ROI."

**Demo script:**
> "This is where Meridian really shines. Instead of a flat list of contacts in your CRM, you see the entire political landscape of the deal. Who has power, who's blocking you, who's your champion — and the AI tells you exactly what's happening and what to do next."

---

### Act 3: Deal Room (Transcripts & Intelligence)

**What to show:** Navigate to "Deal Room" in the sidebar.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Meeting Transcripts** — Upload or record meeting notes. Meridian processes them and extracts key insights, action items, and stakeholder sentiment changes.

2. **Voice Capture** — The mobile FAB (floating action button) at the bottom lets reps capture voice notes on the go, which are transcribed and analyzed.

3. **Intelligence Feed** — A chronological feed of all deal-related activities, automatically tagged and categorized.

**Demo script:**
> "After every customer meeting, the rep just uploads their notes or records a voice memo. Meridian does the rest — it updates the stakeholder map, adjusts the confidence score, and generates the next actions."

---

### Act 4: Ask Meridian (AI Assistant)

**What to show:** Navigate to "Ask Meridian" in the sidebar.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Natural Language Queries** — Ask anything about your pipeline: "Which deals are most likely to close this quarter?" or "What's the common pattern in deals we've lost?"

2. **Cross-Deal Intelligence** — Unlike deal-level AI, Ask Meridian works across your entire pipeline and knowledge base.

**Demo script:**
> "This is your AI sales strategist. It knows every deal, every stakeholder, every meeting note. Ask it anything — it's like having a VP of Sales who never forgets."

---

### Act 5: Stakeholder Directory

**What to show:** Navigate to "Stakeholders" in the sidebar.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Cross-Deal Stakeholder View** — See all contacts across all deals in one place.
2. **Relationship Intelligence** — Understand which stakeholders appear in multiple deals and their influence patterns.

---

### Act 6: Knowledge Base

**What to show:** Navigate to "Knowledge Base" in the sidebar.

**Key talking points:**

1. **Company Profile** — The AI-analyzed company profile from onboarding (products, ICP, sales methodology).
2. **Sales Playbook** — The knowledge base that Meridian uses to generate contextual recommendations.

## Demo Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| Start with the "why" | Before showing features, frame the problem: "Enterprise sales reps spend 60% of their time on admin, not selling. CRM data is always stale. Deal reviews are based on gut feel." |
| Use the stakeholder map | This is the most visually impressive feature. Spend time here. Zoom in, explain the rings, point out the arrows. |
| Show the AI summary | Read the "What's Happening" section aloud. It demonstrates that Meridian understands context, not just data. |
| Toggle language | If demoing to a Chinese audience, switch to ZH mid-demo to show localization. |
| Don't oversell | The product is in early access. Frame it as "this is what we're building" not "this is production-ready." |

## Handling Common Questions

| Question | Suggested Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| "Where does the data come from?" | "Meridian integrates with your CRM, email, and calendar. For this demo, we're using pre-seeded data to show the experience." |
| "How does the AI confidence score work?" | "It's a composite score based on stakeholder engagement, deal velocity, risk factors, and historical patterns. It updates automatically as new information comes in." |
| "How is this different from Gong/Clari?" | "Gong focuses on conversation intelligence. Clari focuses on revenue forecasting. Meridian focuses on the political map of the deal — who has power, who's blocking, and what to do about it." |
| "What's the pricing?" | "We're in early access. Direct them to the Pricing page or say we're working with design partners on a usage-based model." |
| "Can I try it?" | "Absolutely. We can set up a sandbox with your real company data in the onboarding flow." |
